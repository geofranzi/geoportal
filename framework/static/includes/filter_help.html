Help: Filter

Filter by wetland type
Filter wetlands based dominant and/or the emblematic habitat within the mapped area (Natural, Artificial, Natural/Artificial). Artificial sites are dominated by man-made wetland habitats e.g. dams, reservoirs or channels.
Filter by site type
Filter wetlands based on their location to the sea (Coastal, Inland, In/Coastal, Marine). In/Coastal are wetlands covering not only the coastal area, but also the related catchment of a river.
Filter by ecoregion
Ecoregions are areas where ecosystems (and the type, quality, and quantity of environmental resources) are generally similar. Designed to serve as a spatial framework for the research, assessment, and monitoring of ecosystems and ecosystem components, ecoregions denote areas of similarity in the mosaic of biotic, abiotic, terrestrial, and aquatic ecosystem components with humans being considered as part of the biota.
Filter by product or incidcator
Filter wetlands based on available products or indicators.
